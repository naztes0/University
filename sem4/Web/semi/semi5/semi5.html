Crystal Castles

Crystal Castles is a Canadian electronic music duo formed by Ethan Kath. Their sound blends glitchy beats, distorted synths, and haunting vocals, creating a raw and immersive atmosphere. With albums like Crystal Castles I and Crystal Castles II, they gained a cult following, pushing the boundaries of experimental electronic music. Their energetic and chaotic live performances became a signature, captivating audiences worldwide. After Alice Glass left in 2014, Edith Frances took over as vocalist, bringing a new chapter to the band's dark, intense aesthetic.
